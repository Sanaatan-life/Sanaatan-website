Questions

Honest answers.

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime with no penalty. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.
Do the free questions reset every day?
Yes — your 3 free questions reset at midnight every day. There is no need to pay if three questions a day is enough for you.
What scriptures does Sanaatan cover?
Currently the Bhagavad Gita (all 701 verses with Sanskrit, transliteration and English translation) and three principal Upanishads — Isha, Katha, and Mandukya. More Upanishads and the Ramayana are coming soon.
Is the Starter Pack a subscription?
No. The Starter Pack is a one-time payment of ₹99 for 15 questions. There is no recurring charge. Questions do not expire.
Are prices GST inclusive?
Yes. All prices shown include GST. There are no hidden charges or fees.
